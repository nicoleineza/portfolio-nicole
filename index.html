Highlighted Projects

Iteme Shop – Ashesi E-Commerce Platform (Co-Founder)

What began as a course assignment—Campus Market App—grew into Iteme Shop, a Kotlin-based Android e-commerce platform tailored for Ashesi students. After successful deployment and user testing, we evolved the project into a startup and secured Mastercard Foundation funding in 2025.

Key features include user authentication, product listings, in-app messaging, and payment simulations. I led Android development using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Room Database, and a Spring Boot backend. Currently co-developing its business model and technical scalability.

AgroLink – Farmer-to-Buyer Marketplace

Built in a team of three, AgroLink is a Kotlin-based Android platform connecting smallholder farmers to buyers. I led mobile development and integrated Spring Boot APIs for real-time product listings and orders. Emphasis was placed on offline usability and rural accessibility.

Iteme Finance – AI Stock Investment Platform

Co-founded this fintech solution aimed at democratizing stock investment in Rwanda. I helped lead product definition, user research, and pitch development. Our project won 2nd place at Ashesi’s Entrepreneurship Capstone Showcase 2025.
